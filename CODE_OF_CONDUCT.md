# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Participants in the FullFlow project are expected to be respectful, constructive, and professional.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Giving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project
- Helping others learn and contribute

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional engineering environment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
